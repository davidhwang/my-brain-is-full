Frontend as a mature, separate practice both within Drupal and beyond.

Frontend engineering vs 'theming'

A career pathway for 'themers' to be become frontend engs, so the pathway isn't strictly sitebuilder->themer->module dev->system architect

There is room and demand and need for Frontend engs, everywhere, in Drupal and outside, and ultimately that is where your craft and skills lie, whether you continue to work with Drupal or not.
